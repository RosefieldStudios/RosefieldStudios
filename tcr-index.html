What is The Massacre?

The Massacre is a horror/thriller youtube series made by Rosefield Studios. The series tell a story of a entity called "Shadowdrifter" which haunts Rosefield County, there are 2 main parts and 1 sub-part released as of 20.06.2025.

Step 2.

Once you enter the game, you can select a car and start playing!
